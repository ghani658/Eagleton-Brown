Shari Williams

Senior Registered Client Associate
918.359.3345
shari.williams@wfafinet.com

Shari Williams lives by the philosophy that when the clients win, she wins. She’s passionate about partnering with Eagleton Brown Investment Group’s clients on the journey, assisting on their road to success and financial freedom. As a Registered Client Associate, Shari makes sure the clients always come first.

Drawing on her experiences at places like Shearson Lehman Hutton, Smith Barney and Wells Fargo Advisors, Shari brings a higher standard to customer relations as well as sales support & service to Eagleton Brown Investment Group. A native Tulsan, she earned her undergraduate degree in Business & Marketing from University of Oklahoma.

Outside of the office, Shari loves exploring her passions for cooking, gardening, and tennis! If she isn’t enjoying time with her family and friends, she’s bringing her energy and professionalism to the United States Tennis Association, FINRA, and the Kappa Kappa Gamma Alumnae Association. When you see her, ask her about her support for the Porta Caeli House and Clarehouse!
