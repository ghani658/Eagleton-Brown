Mindy Brown, CFP ®

Senior Registered Associate
918.293.7204
mindy.brown@wfafinet.com

Mindy Brown has always been passionate about helping those in need and motivating them to focus on long term goals to help them get closer to financial freedom. At Eagleton Brown Investment Group, Mindy’s found her dream job in this profession. As a Senior Registered Associate, she gets to help people every day by giving tailored advice according to their needs & resources. Mindy defines success in her position by helping Eagleton Brown Investment Group’s clients understand the importance of saving and investing in a way they can implement.

Before entering the financial services industry, she graduated from Oklahoma State University in December 2004 with a Bachelor’s degree in Finance. Soon after, she accepted her first job with A.G. Edwards, which later became Wells Fargo Advisors. In 2011, she and her husband Kent teamed up with Paul & Jim Eagleton to form Eagleton Brown Investment Group. A lifelong learner, Mindy also earned her CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ Certification that year to help towards Eagleton Brown Investment Group mission of providing exceptional service and advice that meets their client’s needs.

Outside of the office, you’ll find Mindy spending time with her husband (and co-worker) Kent along with their two children Preston and Reagan. She’s a lifelong native of Oklahoma and originally hails from Broken Arrow. The family has been attending the Church at Battle Creek in Broken Arrow for over 16 years.
